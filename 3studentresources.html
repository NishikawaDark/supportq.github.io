Self-Care & Coping

Grounding Techniques

When you feel overwhelmed, try one of these to bring yourself back to the present moment.

🌬️ Deep breathing 🧘 Body scan 🚶 Short walk 📓 Journaling 🎵 Music 💬 Talk it out

Mindfulness & Breathing

A quick breathing exercise can calm your nervous system in under two minutes. Try the 4-7-8 technique: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, then exhale slowly for 8.
